4-H Badge Displayer

The 4-H Badge Displayer will access publicly shared badges from the 4-H Badging Infrastructure and the Mozilla OBI and display them for the earner. Badge display plays a crucial role in the 4-H Badges ecosystem as part of a badgeâ€™s value resides in its usage and social currency. This is a factor that is affected by where and how badges are displayed. While social networking sites, discipline-oriented community portals, and job search sites, as well as personal sites and blogs are all potential badge displayers, the 4-H Badge Displayer will be unique to the larger 4-H community across the United States and possibly the world.

Why Display Badges?

  • Build comprehensive profiles
    Enhance profiles with more granular skill and competency information.
  • Build community and social capital
    Help learners find mentors and peers with similar interests. Badges help reinforce camaraderie, synthesize teams and build communities of practice.
  • Understand users
    Badges provide evidence-backed data that can be used to better understand user skills, interests, history and goals.
