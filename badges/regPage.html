Welcome to the 4-H Digital Badge system!

NOTE: The local and state 4-H program must register with this site before it can be used by youth, parents, or adult volunteers in 4-H. Any other user registration would be as a General User and would not have access to 4-H Badges.

This website is designed to provide a safe and secure environment for children and youth to earn badges through 4-H. The process requires local 4-H staff to register in the system and serve as a moderator for digital badges in their county. Local volunteer leaders in 4-H will work with local 4-H staff to issue badges to young people involved in their club, group or project. Young people ages 13 and older will simply register as a user. Children under the age of 13 will register with their parent's or guardian's assistance and permission. To begin, choose the link below that fits you best.

Local 4-H Agent, Extension Employee, Or University Faculty

Critical Instructions to Extension Staff!

If you are a local 4-H employee interested in beginning to use the 4-H Digital Badge system for a local county level 4-H program, please follow the steps listed below. The 4-H Digital Badge support team will assist each state and county in using the site through state and local level contacts.

  • IMPORTANT: Contact the 4-H Digital Badges Support Team and let them know your state wants to begin using the site.
    • - A state level contact must be identified and then register with the site.
    - The State 4-H Badges Contact will then be activated or approved by the System Administrator of the 4-H Digital Badge System.
  • Once the State 4-H Badges Contact is authorized local 4-H staff should register
    • - State level contacts will authorize Local 4-H Badges Contacts
  • Once Local 4-H Badges Contacts are authorized Local Adult Volunteer Leaders should register.
    • - Local 4-H Badges Contacts will authorize volunteer leaders to operate within the system.

NOTE: Local approval of a volunteer leader is critical as this step assures that adult users have appropriate permissions to interact with young people in their club, group or project. Approval by the local staff is done according to state 4-H volunteer guidelines and procedures.

Following the steps outlined above will make the process of getting local volunteer leaders and youth started in the 4-H Digital Badge System much smoother. As Volunteer Leaders register the local 4-H Contact will be notified via e-mail and respond accordingly to approve the leader. If you recieve a registration notice from a user you do not know, please verify who they are before approving them. Again, this is critical in that your authorization indicates that the adult user is authorized to interact with young people in your 4-H program. Registering users before these steps may result in some delay in ability to use the system.

You may find the training videos at this link helpful in creating new accounts: Orientation and Training

NOTE: When youth under the age of 13 register the process will involve their parent or guardian. You will receive e-mail notices that parents or guardians have registered. Parents and guardians will confirm that their child has permission to use the system.

NOTE: When youth age 13 or older register the Local 4-H Contact will receive a notification that they have registered.

Local 4-H Badges Contacts will only recieve notifcations of user registrations within their assigned county and approve of those users accordingly.

Contact 4-H Digital Badges at 4hdigitalbadges@gmail.com or your State 4-H Badges Contact if you need assistance.

Volunteer Leader Or Teacher Users

Instructions for Volunteer Leaders or Teachers!

4-H Digital Badge Survey - Adult Notification - Instructions about the survey will be provided as you continue into the system

At this point you can begin to register. You will select the Create New Account tab and begin. Once you have completed the registration form and pressed submit a message will be sent to your e-mail address welcoming you to the system. Parent Or Guardian Of Child Under Age 13

Instructions for Parents!

If you have a child under the age of 13 interested in 4-H digital badges then the following information is important to you. According to the Child Online Privacy and Protection Act (COPPA) you must give permission for your child to register as a user in the 4-H Digital Badge System. The registration process requires that you register as a user first and then complete or assist with your child's registration.

Also, the 4-H Digital Badge System is under continuing improvement. User input is important and users are invited to participate in a short survey. Please click on the link below to see information about the survey as a Parent of a child who may be asked to respond.

4-H Digital Badge Survey - Parental Approval - Instructions about the survey will be provided as you continue into the system.

Continue to Create an Account to complete registration. You will select the Create New Account tab and begin. Once you have completed the registration form and pressed submit a message will be sent to your e-mail address welcoming you to the system. An administrator of the system will confirm and activitate your registration as an approved Parent at which time you will receive a second message about completing your registration. You will follow the link in that message to confirm your account and set your password. At that point you can begin to use the system.

You may find the training videos at this link helpful in creating new accounts: Orientation and Training

Contact 4-H Digital Badges at 4hdigitalbadges@gmail.com or your State 4-H Badges Contact if you need assistance.
