Documentation on 4-H Digital Badges

Documents listed below are related to the development of the 4-H Digital Badge System. This project is a collaborative effort between Auburn University as the lead university for development of the technical aspects and its eXtension leadership, the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in regard to content and criteria development and assessment, the U.S. Department of Agriculture, National 4-H Headquarters as liaison to the 4-H community and federal partner, and National 4-H Council in regard to the initial curriculum used and future endeavors.
