Volunteer Leader Or Teacher Users

Instructions for Volunteer Leaders or Teachers!

4-H Digital Badge Survey - Adult Notification - Instructions about the survey will be provided as you continue into the system

At this point you can begin to register. You will select the Create New Account tab and begin. Once you have completed the registration form and pressed submit a message will be sent to your e-mail address welcoming you to the system. An administrator of the system will confirm and activitate your registration as an approved Volunteer Leader at which time you will receive a second message about completing your registration. You will follow the link in that message to confirm your account and set your password. At that point you can begin to use the system. Please go to Create a New Account.

You may find the training videos at this link helpful in creating new accounts: Orientation and Training

If you will be working with a group of youth please contact your local 4-H Staff for details regarding working with a group. Thank you for registering as an adult volunteer or teacher involved with 4-H.

Contact 4-H Digital Badges at 4hdigitalbadges@gmail.com or your State 4-H Badges Contact if you need assistance.
