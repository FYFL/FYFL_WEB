Welcome to the 4-H Digital Badge system!

NOTE: The local and state 4-H program must register with this site before it can be used by youth, parents, or adult volunteers in 4-H. Any other user registration would be as a General User and would not have acces to 4-H Badges.

This website is designed to provide a safe and secure environment for children and youth to earn badges through 4-H. The process requires local 4-H staff to register in the system and serve as a moderator for digital badges in their county. Local volunteer leaders in 4-H will work with local 4-H staff to issue badges to young people involved in their club, group or project. Young people ages 13 and older will simply register as a user. Children under the age of 13 will register with their parent's or guardian's assistance and persmission. To begin, choose the link below that fits you best.

User Levels

Local 4-H Agent, Extension Employee, or University Faculty

Volunteer Leader or Teacher involved with or interested in 4-H

Parent or Guardian of Child under age 13 who is interested in earning badges in 4-H

Child or young person interested in earning digital badges in 4-H
