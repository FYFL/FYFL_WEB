Badges Introduced in 2014

The 4-H Digital Badges System introduces five new badges in its debut! Those badges are in the area of robotics as shown below. The first three badges come from the 4-H Junk Drawer Robotics curriculum. The fourth badge is about the various ty pes of robot kits (Lego NXT, TETRIX, CEENBoT, VEX, etc.) that young people may be involved with. The fifth badge is about competitions which can vary but represent competencies in teamwork and professionalism bu being part of a competition team. To learn more about each badge just click on the link above the one you are interested in to go its badge page.

To earn a badge you must register as a 4-H Digital Badges System user. When you register as a user you will also need to identify an adult leader as your facilitator. They will be the ones to review your work to determine if it meets the criteria set forth on the badge page. To request a badge you may simply click on the link on the badge page you are interested in to go to the “Earn This Badge” page for that badge. Follow the instructions there to have the badge issued to you.
